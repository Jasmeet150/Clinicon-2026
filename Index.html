About CLINICON 2026

A landmark medical student summit celebrating innovation, education, collaboration, culture, and leadership in medicine.

What is CLINICON?

CLINICON 2026 is a multi-dimensional medical summit designed to bring together students, faculty, clinicians, researchers, and institutions through academic excellence and student engagement.

Celebrating the Faculty

The summit commemorates the Anniversary of the Establishment of the Faculty of Medicine and reflects the journey of growth, achievement, and academic distinction.

Three Pillars

Academic Excellence • Sports & Recreation • Community & Culture

Organized By

Organized and coordinated by the Student Medical Association in collaboration with faculty mentors and institutional leadership.
